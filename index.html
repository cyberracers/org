Play-to-Earn Racing Experience

Cyber Racers offers an exciting Play-to-Earn experience where players engage in various PvE and PvP racing modes, including Lap Races, Time Trials, Police Escapes, Demolition Races, and more. Built on real vehicle physics with high-performance cars, the game delivers immersive gameplay while allowing players to earn CRS tokens and NFT rewards.

learn More

Robust NFT Asset System

The game features over 7,800 unique Car NFTs, 900 Racetrack Blueprint NFTs, and rare utility NFTs like Gas Stations, Workshops, and Car Rentals. These NFTs can be traded or used to generate passive income through leasing, repair fees, and race commissions — making them valuable digital assets in the metaverse.

learn More

Decentralized Economic Ecosystem

Cyber Racers is powered by the CRS token with a unique 5% transaction tax model: 2% is redistributed to holders, 2% is burned, and 1% goes to the community treasury managed via DAO. Combined with social gameplay features like Racing Clubs and Crew Battles, this creates a sustainable, player-driven economic system on the blockchain.
