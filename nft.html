TRADE NFTs

Cyber Racers has its very own native marketplace that is a host to a wide range of NFT collections based on racing. Each NFT in the marketplace can be used to either trade an NFT, exchange for rewards in the game, and much more. Apart from the trading aspect, NFTs in Cyber Racers can also be used to generate income streams within the game. Players and members are given options like renting out their Car-based NFTs to earn CRS tokens in exchange.

The biggest difference that sets Cyber Racers from other community-based games on the blockchain is the ability of each member to create a unique persona for themselves. With the in-game rewards and opportunities, each member can level up accordingly and earn more with resources that are available within the game.

CARS

Own high-performance, customizable vehicles with unique models, skins, and upgrade potential. Car NFTs are the foundation of your racing career and in-game progression

TRACKS

Collect racetrack blueprint NFTs and earn CRS from races hosted on your track. Each blueprint represents a unique location and passive income opportunity

STATIONS

Fuel is essential. Gas Station NFTs let you earn gas fees from every racer who refuels, making you a key infrastructure owner in the Cyber Racers ecosystem.

WORKSHOPS

Hold workshop NFTs to upgrade, maintain, and fine-tune vehicles. Earn rewards through player service fees in a racing economy driven by performance.

RENTALS

Let others race your cars. Car Rental NFTs generate income by allowing your vehicles to be used by other players, expanding your earning network.

COLLECTIONS

A dynamic NFT ecosystem with diverse utilities—from racing and building to earning and trading. The rarer the asset, the higher your potential rewards.
